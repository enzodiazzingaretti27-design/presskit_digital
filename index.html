01 Biography

The Sound of Concrete & Steel.

KEXXY emerged from Mendoza's underground rave circuit — a raw, unfiltered force built on the philosophy that the floor is the only judge, and the bass is the only truth.

Blending hardcore techno, industrial noise, and dark electronics, KEXXY's sets are renowned for their relentless intensity and visceral precision. No compromise. No filters. Pure machine logic translated into physical force.

From underground warehouse raves in Cuyo to international festival stages, KEXXY has cemented a reputation as one of the most uncompromising acts in South America's underground electronics scene. A relentless touring schedule across Argentina and beyond has built a devoted following through nothing but raw energy and a zero-compromise approach to sound.
